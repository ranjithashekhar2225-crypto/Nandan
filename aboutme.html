    About Me

    Over the last decade, Nandan has worked in varied roles across impact investing, sustainability, consulting, non-profits, academia and politics in Asia, Africa and Europe. Based in the United Kingdom, he is currently a Programme Lead at Climate Catalyst, an international non-profit start-up dedicated to galvanising the collective power of businesses, investors and civil society to influence and accelerate policy change.
